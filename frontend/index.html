Island Essentials

Everything You Need to Know

Quick answers to the questions every first-time visitor asks.

🌡️

Best Time to Visit

December to April is peak season — sunny, dry, and warm. May to November offers lower rates and fewer crowds, with the occasional tropical rain.

✈️

Getting Here

Fly direct to North Eleuthera (ELH) from Miami or Nassau. Take a 10-min taxi to the dock, then a 10-min ferry to the island. Or arrive by boat to Valentines Marina.

💰

Budget & Money

Harbour Island is expensive by Bahamian standards. Budget $250–$800/night for accommodation. The US dollar is widely accepted; credit cards work at most businesses.

🛵

Getting Around

No cars — the island is golf-cart only. Rentals run $60–80/day. Everything is walkable from Dunmore Town, though a cart makes the Pink Sand Beach easy.

📡

For Mariners

Monitor VHF Channel 16 on approach. Valentines Marina guides boats in daily. Controlling depth ~6-7ft MLW in the approach channel — call ahead if you draw over 5.5ft.

🌿

Eco Reminders

Reef-safe sunscreen only (Bahamian law). Take nothing from the beach. Observe turtles from 30 feet. The reef is healthy because locals and visitors protect it.
