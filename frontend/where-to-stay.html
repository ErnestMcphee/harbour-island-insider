⚓ Full Marina 🤿 PADI Dive Centre 🏖️ Beach Shuttle 🍽️ Waterfront Restaurant 🚤 Water Sports 🎣 Fishing Charters 📡 VHF 16 ⚡ 30/50/100A Power

Valentines Resort & Marina is the anchor of Harbour Island's marine community and our unequivocal top recommendation for most visitors. Whether you're arriving by boat or by plane, Valentines offers the most complete island experience in one location.

The marina has welcomed cruisers for over 30 years and accommodates vessels from small day boats to 160-foot superyachts. The PADI-certified dive centre offers daily reef dives, courses from Discover Scuba to Divemaster, and guided trips to all the best local sites including the legendary Current Cut drift dive. The waterfront restaurant serves fresh-caught local fish, traditional Bahamian cracked conch, and craft cocktails with harbour views.

Hotel rooms and marina villas are tastefully appointed with tropical furnishings and private patios. A complimentary golf-cart shuttle runs to Pink Sand Beach regularly, and the marina's concierge team can arrange virtually anything — fishing charters, airport transfers, provisioning runs, and custom island excursions.

Who is Valentines best for?

⚓ Boaters

The only full-service marina on the island. 50+ slips, fuel dock, pump-out, laundry, Wi-Fi, dock hands 7 days a week.

🤿 Divers

PADI dive centre on property, best local knowledge, and direct access to Current Cut and 7 major dive sites.

👨‍👩‍👧 Families

Spacious villas, supervised children's snorkel programmes, beach shuttle, and activities for all ages.

💑 Couples

Sunset harbour views from your villa patio, anniversary packages, waterfront dining, and the most romantic beach on earth a short drive away.

Pink Sands Resort

Boutique Luxury
★★★★★
🏖️ Beachfront 🍽️ Restaurant 🏊 Pool 💆 Spa

Pink Sands Resort sits directly on the Atlantic side with pink sand beach frontage — arguably the best beach location on the island. The 25 cottages are beautifully designed with a laid-back luxury feel. Strong restaurant and beautiful pool area.

Best for: Those whose primary goal is the Pink Sand Beach, couples seeking a romantic retreat, or guests who don't need marina or dive facilities.

What's missing vs Valentines: No marina (you can't arrive by boat), no dive centre on property, fewer activities and services. Excellent for beach lovers, but less versatile.

💡 Insider Tip

If you want beach frontage AND diving, we recommend Valentines — the 10-minute beach shuttle is genuinely convenient, and having the PADI dive centre steps from your room is worth more than a beachfront view in our opinion.

Holiday Rentals & Private Villas

Self-Catering
Various prices

Harbour Island has a healthy supply of private villa and cottage rentals, ranging from modest local homes to spectacular oceanfront properties. Vrbo and Airbnb both have listings. This can be the most cost-effective option for families or groups staying a week or more.

What to keep in mind: Most rentals don't include activities, transfers, or local expertise. You'll need to arrange your own diving, fishing, and beach access. For most first-time visitors, we find the concierge services and activities at Valentines represent better overall value even at a higher nightly rate.

Our verdict: Great for returning visitors who know the island, families on extended stays, or those who want a completely self-directed experience.

Marina Berths: Arriving by Boat

For Liveaboards & Cruisers
⚓ Valentines Marina

If you're arriving by boat, Valentines Marina is the only full-service marina on Harbour Island. There are no other viable alternatives for transient vessels. Alternatives include anchoring in the bay (good holding, 6–8ft MLW) or the more remote Royal Island anchorage, but you'll need a dinghy to access town.

Valentines accommodates vessels from 20 to 160 feet. Call ahead on VHF 16 or phone to reserve your slip — peak season (December–April) books out. Full marina guide with waypoints, depths, and facilities: The Cruiser's Guide to Valentines Marina →
