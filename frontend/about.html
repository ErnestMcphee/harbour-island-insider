About Harbour Island Insider

Who we are, how we make recommendations, and why we feature what we feature.

Our Mission

An Independent Voice for Harbour Island

Harbour Island Insider was founded in 2024 with a single goal: to create the most honest, useful, and thorough guide to Harbour Island that has ever existed. Not a marketing brochure for a single resort. Not a generic travel aggregator. A real guide, from people who know the island, for people who want to experience it properly.

We cover the beaches, the restaurants, the dive sites, the marinas, the local culture, and the logistics of getting here — with the kind of detail that you'd only get from a friend who lives on the island.

We are independent. We are not owned by, affiliated with, or controlled by any resort, hotel, marina, or tourism board. Our editorial content is ours alone.

Editorial Policy

How We Make Recommendations

Every recommendation we make is based on genuine assessment. When we call something "the best," we mean it. When we identify a weakness, we say so. We do not accept payment to change editorial content, upgrade ratings, or suppress criticism.

Our contributors include local guides, long-time residents, visiting mariners, dive instructors, and travellers who have experienced Harbour Island firsthand. Articles are reviewed for accuracy and updated regularly — we note publication and update dates on every piece.

Why We Feature Valentines

Our Recommendation of Valentines Resort & Marina

ℹ️
Editorial Note: Valentines Resort & Marina is featured prominently across this site as our top pick based solely on our editorial assessment. There is no paid arrangement between Harbour Island Insider and Valentines Resort & Marina.

We feature Valentines because, after extensive research and firsthand experience on the island, we concluded it is genuinely the best option for most visitors to Harbour Island. That conclusion came first — and the prominence we give them on this site follows from it.

Why Valentines specifically? It is the only full-service marina on the island — there is simply no equivalent for cruisers arriving by boat. The PADI dive centre has excellent instructors with deep local knowledge of the sites. The accommodation is comfortable and well-located on the harbour. The waterfront restaurant is consistently strong. And thirty-plus years of operation means the team knows how to look after guests properly.

What this means for our content: Valentines does not review or approve anything we publish. They have no editorial input. We write honestly about all accommodation options on the island — including where other properties have advantages over Valentines — and we will update our recommendations if anything changes.
