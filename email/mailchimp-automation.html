Our goal: build a list of ocean lovers who trust our recommendations — and who book Valentines Resort & Marina through our affiliate link when they're ready to visit.

Step 1 Create Your Mailchimp Account

👤 Action Required — Takes ~10 minutes
  1. Go to mailchimp.com → "Sign Up Free"
  2. Use your blog email: editor@harbourislandinsider.com (or your preferred address)
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete setup wizard: choose "Create a campaign" → skip for now

Step 2 Create Your Audience

👤 Action Required
  1. Mailchimp Dashboard → Audience → Manage Audience → Create Audience
  2. Audience name: The Island Dispatch
  3. From name: Harbour Island Insider
  4. From email: editor@harbourislandinsider.com
  5. Reminder: "You signed up for island travel tips and conditions updates at harbourislandinsider.com"
  6. Enable GDPR fields (recommended for EU visitors)
  7. Save → note your Audience ID (Settings → Audience name and defaults)

Merge Tags to Add (Audience → Settings → Audience fields and *|MERGE|* tags)

Step 3 Get API Key & Configure WordPress Plugin

👤 Action Required
  1. Mailchimp → Account icon (top right) → Account → Extras → API Keys
  2. Click "Create A Key" — name it "Harbour Island Insider WordPress"
  3. Copy the key (it looks like: abc123def456-us1)
  4. In WordPress: Settings → Valentines Resort → Mailchimp API Key → paste key
  5. Paste your Audience ID in the List ID field
  6. Save settings

Step 5 Set Up Automation Flows

Flow 1 — Welcome Sequence (Triggered on Subscribe)

Set up via: Automations → Classic Automations → Welcome new subscribers

1

Email 1 — "Welcome to the Island Journal! 🌴"

Sends: Immediately on subscribe

Subject: Welcome aboard, *|FNAME|* — your Harbour Island guide is ready
Preview text: Pink sand, clear water, honest advice — delivered monthly.
Content: Welcome, what to expect, link to the 5 most popular articles, Valentines booking card (subtle — "where we recommend staying"). Use Template 1 from email-templates.html.
CTA: "Read our most popular article →" (link to marina guide or pink sand guide)

2

Email 2 — "The Island Secret Most Visitors Miss"

Sends: Day 3 after subscribe

Subject: The Harbour Island tip that changes everything, *|FNAME|*
Preview text: Sunrise on Pink Sand Beach — and why you need to be there.
Content: Feature the Pink Sand Beach sunrise tip, link to the beach guide article. Mid-email: introduce Valentines as "where we recommend basing yourself — 5-min shuttle to the beach, included." CTA to Valentines booking page.
Goal: Drive first Valentines booking click.

3

Email 3 — Segmented: Mariners OR Hotel Guests

Sends: Day 7 after subscribe

If Mariner: Subject: "Your complete marina briefing for Harbour Island" → Send marina guide content + Valentines slip booking CTA
If Hotel/Unsure: Subject: "Where to stay on Harbour Island — our honest verdict" → Send where-to-stay guide + Valentines room booking CTA
Setup: Create two versions of Email 3 and use Mailchimp's "Send to segment" option for each.

4

Email 4 — "This is what waiting too long costs you"

Sends: Day 14 after subscribe (only if no Valentines link clicked yet)

Subject: *|FNAME|*, peak season slips go fast — here's what to know
Preview text: Harbour Island's marina books out December through April.
Content: Urgency-driven email about marina/hotel availability in peak season. Not pushy — frame as a helpful heads-up from a friend. Feature the Valentines booking process (no payment to enquire, 2-hour response). Strong CTA.
Condition: Only send if subscriber has NOT clicked a Valentines link in emails 1–3.

5

Email 5 — "Before you go: the Harbour Island packing list"

Sends: Day 21 after subscribe

Subject: The packing list every Harbour Island visitor needs (don't forget #3)
Preview text: Reef-safe sunscreen is Bahamian law. Here's everything else.
Content: Genuinely useful packing/preparation list. Friendly, editorial tone. Footer: "Still haven't booked? Valentines Marina and Resort has a few April/May slots remaining. → Check availability" Keep it low-key — the relationship is now established.
Goal: Final gentle nudge, then transition to monthly newsletter cadence.
