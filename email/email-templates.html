Getting to Harbour Island

By Air: Fly into North Eleuthera Airport (ELH) — direct flights from Miami, Nassau, and Fort Lauderdale. A taxi to the ferry dock takes 5 minutes ($5–8), and the ferry to Harbour Island runs every 20 minutes ($5 each way).

By Boat: Call us on VHF Channel 16 or +1 (242) 333-2142 when you're one hour out. Our dock staff will be ready.

From Nassau: Fast ferry service runs several times weekly, or Fly from Nassau to ELH on Flamingo Air or Southern Air.

🎒 Don't Forget to Pack
  • Reef-safe sunscreen (biodegradable — required by Bahamian law)
  • Passport or government ID (required for Bahamas entry)
  • Light layers for evenings (the trade winds can be refreshing)
  • Snorkel mask if you have one (or rent from our dive centre)
  • Cash in small denominations — some local shops are cash-only

Need to arrange anything before you arrive? Airport transfers, provisioning requests, dive bookings, special occasion packages — just reply to this email or call us.
